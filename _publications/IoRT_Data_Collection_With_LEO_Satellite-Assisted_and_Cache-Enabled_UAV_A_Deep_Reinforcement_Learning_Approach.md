This paper considers a space-air-ground integrated network for Internet of Remote Things data collection, where a UAV with a cache node collects data and forwards it through a LEO satellite backhaul. A deep reinforcement learning algorithm is proposed to jointly optimize UAV trajectory, IoRT device association, and data caching policy.
